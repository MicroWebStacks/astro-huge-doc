# Math Demo

Inline math now renders directly in normal prose, for example
$E = mc^2$, $a^2 + b^2 = c^2$, and
$\operatorname{Var}(X) = \mathbb{E}[X^2] - \mathbb{E}[X]^2$.

## Quadratic Formula

$$
x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}
$$

## Gaussian Density

$$
f(x) = \frac{1}{\sigma \sqrt{2\pi}}
\exp\left(-\frac{(x - \mu)^2}{2\sigma^2}\right)
$$

## Matrix Example

$$
A =
\begin{bmatrix}
1 & 2 & 3 \\
0 & 1 & 4 \\
0 & 0 & 1
\end{bmatrix}
$$

## Mixed Text And Formulas

When $n$ grows, the harmonic series
$$
\sum_{k=1}^{n} \frac{1}{k}
$$
diverges, while
$$
\sum_{k=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{k^2} = \frac{\pi^2}{6}
$$
converges.
